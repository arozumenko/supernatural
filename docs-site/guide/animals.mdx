# Animals

Supernatural features 20 animal species organized into 5 tiers. Animals use the same priority-based utility AI decision engine as agents, with species-specific weights, 3-sense awareness (sight, sound, smell), and special abilities.

## The Food Chain

Animals form a complete food chain:

- **Apex predators** hunt mid predators and large herbivores
- **Mid predators** hunt small prey and medium herbivores
- **Large herbivores** graze and flee from predators
- **Medium herbivores** graze and have moderate defense
- **Small prey** forage and rely on speed or special defenses

Agents sit at the top of the food chain but start vulnerable. An unarmed agent can be killed by apex predators and even overwhelmed by pack-hunting wolves.

## Animal AI

Each animal species uses a utility AI system with:

### Three-Sense Awareness

- **Sight** -- Detection range varies by species; predators see farther
- **Sound** -- Alerts nearby animals to threats; herds use warning calls
- **Smell** -- Tracks food sources and detects predators downwind

### Decision Priorities

Animals follow the same priority structure as agents:
1. **Flee** from threats (predators, armed agents)
2. **Fight** if cornered or defending territory/young
3. **Eat** when hungry
4. **Drink** when thirsty
5. **Socialize** (herd/pack behavior, flocking)
6. **Wander** and explore

### Flocking Behavior

Herd and pack animals exhibit flocking:
- **Alignment** -- Move in the same direction as nearby group members
- **Cohesion** -- Stay close to the group center
- **Separation** -- Avoid crowding neighbors

## Pack Defense

Pack animals (wolves, dogs) coordinate attacks:

- When one pack member is threatened, nearby members respond
- Pack hunting allows taking down larger prey
- Alpha animals lead the group and set patrol routes

Herd animals (deer, cows, horses) use **sentinel alertness** -- one member watches while others graze, and warning calls trigger group flight.

## Taming

Agents can tame animals through repeated positive interactions. Taming mechanics:

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Max tamed animals per agent | 5 |
| Taming chance | Scales with CHA stat and Social skill |
| Production interval | Configurable per species |

### Tamed Animal Benefits

Tamed animals provide three types of benefit:

1. **Follow** -- The animal follows the agent around the world
2. **Defend** -- Tamed animals fight alongside the agent when threatened
3. **Produce** -- Species-specific resource production:
   - Cows produce milk
   - Chickens lay eggs
   - Sheep produce wool
   - Goats produce milk

### Taming Tips

- High CHA and Social skill dramatically improve taming success
- The **Social** archetype has the "Animal Whisperer" strategy rule that boosts taming
- Start with small, docile animals (chickens, sheep) before attempting larger ones
- Tamed animals that die are lost permanently -- protect them from predators

## Animal Spawning

Animal populations are managed by the server:

- **Initial spawn** -- Configurable count per species based on world config
- **Population thresholds** -- Animals respawn when populations drop below minimums
- **Day/night cycle** -- Some species (cats, foxes, rats, hedgehogs) are more active at night
- **Breeding** -- Animals can reproduce when population is below cap and conditions are met
