# World and Survival

Supernatural worlds are procedurally generated tile maps (default 120x90 tiles, 32px each) with diverse biomes, resources, and hazards. Agents must continuously manage 7 needs to stay alive.

![World biomes](/images/world-biomes.png)

## World Generation

The world is generated in 9 passes, producing a mix of terrain types including grassland, forest, desert, swamp, mountain, water, beach, and more. Each world contains:

- **Trees** -- Primary wood source, regrow over time
- **Rocks** -- Stone and iron deposits
- **Plants** -- Berries, mushrooms, herbs, flowers, and poison shrooms
- **Water tiles** -- Required for drinking
- **Iron deposits** -- 15-20 per world, never respawn (extremely scarce)

The 24 tile types include grass, dirt, sand, water (shallow and deep), forest floor, mountain, snow, swamp, lava, ice, farmland, road, bridge, and tombstone (placed on permadeath).

## Needs System

Agents have 7 needs, each ranging from 0 to 100. Needs decay every tick (10 ticks per second) and must be restored through specific actions.

![Agent needs](/images/agents-needs.png)

| Need | Decay Rate | Restored By | Critical Threshold |
|------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Protein Hunger | 0.06/tick | Meat, cooked meat, fish | < 30 |
| Plant Hunger | 0.06/tick | Berries, mushrooms, herbs | < 30 |
| Thirst | Constant | Drinking at a water tile | < 30 |
| Stamina | Per action | Resting (+4.5/tick when resting) | < 15 |
| Health | Damage-based | Resting, met needs, toughness stat | < 30 |
| Social | Slow decay | Interacting with other agents | < 40 |
| Shelter | Exposure decay | Being near structures (campfire, walls, tent) | < 30 |

### Starvation and Damage

When needs hit zero, the agent takes continuous damage:

| Condition | Damage |
|-----------|--------|
| Empty protein hunger | -0.5 HP/tick |
| Empty plant hunger | -0.5 HP/tick |
| Both hungers empty | -1.5 HP/tick (compounding) |
| Dehydration (thirst = 0) | -0.8 HP/tick |
| Exposure (shelter = 0) | -0.15 HP/tick |

### Health Regeneration

Health recovers through multiple channels:

- **Resting** -- Active healing while performing rest action
- **Passive** -- Slow background healing when not in danger
- **Toughness bonus** -- Scales with the agent's TGH stat
- **Needs-met bonus** -- Additional healing when all needs are above critical thresholds

## Activity Metabolism

Different actions consume stamina at different rates. The `activityMultipliers` in the world config scale stamina drain per action type:

| Activity | Multiplier |
|----------|-----------|
| Fighting | 1.8x |
| Running/Fleeing | 1.5x |
| Mining | 1.4x |
| Woodcutting | 1.3x |
| Building | 1.2x |
| Walking | 1.0x |
| Foraging | 0.8x |
| Socializing | 0.6x |
| Resting | 0.4x |

## Resources and Regrowth

### Trees
Trees are the primary source of wood. When harvested, they drop wood and occasionally seeds. Trees regrow over time based on the `trees.growthRate` config value.

### Rocks and Iron
Stone is abundant but iron is the scarcest resource in the game. Each world spawns only 15-20 iron deposits and they **never respawn**. An agent with iron tools and weapons has a massive combat and crafting advantage.

### Plants
Plants provide food for the plant hunger need. Each plant type restores different amounts:

| Plant Type | Nutrition Value | Notes |
|-----------|----------------|-------|
| Berry Bush | Medium | Safe, common |
| Mushroom | Medium | Safe, found in forests |
| Herbs | Low-Medium | Also used in crafting |
| Flowers | Low | Minimal nutrition |
| Poison Shroom | Negative | Deals poison damage |

Plants regrow at rates defined in the world config. Poison shrooms look similar to regular mushrooms, and eating one triggers the `poison` death cause.

## Death Causes

The game tracks 9 distinct death causes, each triggering different [genome mutations](/docs/agent-system#death-and-evolution):

| Death Cause | Trigger |
|-------------|---------|
| `starvation_protein` | Protein hunger stayed at 0 |
| `starvation_plant` | Plant hunger stayed at 0 |
| `starvation_both` | Both hungers at 0 simultaneously |
| `dehydration` | Thirst at 0 for extended period |
| `killed_by_animal` | Animal dealt the killing blow |
| `killed_by_agent` | Another agent dealt the killing blow |
| `exhaustion` | Stamina depletion leading to collapse |
| `poison` | Ate a poison shroom |
| `exposure` | Shelter need at 0 for too long |
