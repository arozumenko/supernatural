# Crafting and Building

Supernatural has 52 items and 53 recipes spread across 4 material tiers. Crafting progression gates access to better tools, weapons, armor, and structures.

## Material Tiers

| Tier | Materials | Unlocks |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Hand** | Bare hands | Basic gathering (slow), punching |
| **Wood** | Wood planks, sticks | Club, wooden walls, campfire, basic shelter |
| **Stone** | Stone, flint | Stone axe, stone pickaxe, stone tools, better weapons |
| **Bone/Hide** | Animal bones, leather | Bow, arrows, leather armor, rope, advanced tools |
| **Iron** | Iron ingots | Iron sword, iron shield, reinforced walls, best-in-class gear |

Each tier represents a significant power increase. A stone axe harvests wood faster than bare hands, and an iron sword deals 2.5x the damage of fists.

## Iron Scarcity

Iron is the rarest and most impactful resource in the game:

- **15-20 deposits** per world generation
- **Never respawn** once mined
- An agent with iron gear has a **massive advantage** in combat and resource gathering
- The Builder archetype has "Iron Seeker" and "Protect Investment" strategy rules
- The Warrior archetype has "Iron Rush" to prioritize finding iron early

Iron creates a natural arms race -- agents who secure iron deposits first gain a lasting edge. LLM gods can use [genome patches](/docs/llm-gods) to adjust iron-seeking priority.

## Structures

Structures provide the **shelter** need and strategic advantages:

| Structure | Materials | Shelter Value | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Campfire | 3 Wood, 1 Flint | Low | Also used for cooking |
| Wooden Wall | 4 Wood Plank | Medium | Basic perimeter |
| Wooden Door | 3 Wood Plank | Medium | Controls access |
| Tent | 4 Stick, 2 Leather | Medium-High | Portable shelter |
| Reinforced Wall | 2 Wood, 1 Iron | High | Strongest protection |

Structures decay over time and can be destroyed by other agents. The Builder archetype focuses on constructing and maintaining shelters.

## Crafting Skills

The Crafting and Building skills affect:

- **Crafting speed** -- Higher skill = faster item creation
- **Building efficiency** -- Higher skill = less material waste
- **Recipe access** -- Some complex recipes require minimum skill levels
- **Harvest bonuses** -- Woodcutting and Mining skills increase yields from trees and rocks

Skill bonuses for harvesting are configured in the `harvest` section of the world config:

```json
{
  "harvest": {
    "baseWoodAmount": 2,
    "baseStoneAmount": 2,
    "baseIronAmount": 1,
    "skillBonusPerLevel": 0.02
  }
}
```

## Cooking

Raw meat can cause food poisoning. Cooking at a campfire or cooking spit:

- Removes poison risk
- Increases nutrition value
- Requires the Survival skill (higher level = better results)
- The Survivor archetype has the "Cook Everything" strategy rule
